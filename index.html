When Fog is on, moving a Player token into an area permanently reveals it, blocked by any walls and closed doors in the way. Monster/NPC tokens never reveal fog.

Map, tokens, walls, and fog sync automatically while you're in a Room above — everyone in the room sees the same board. Player vision is blocked by walls and closed doors (real line-of-sight via ray casting).
